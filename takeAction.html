Take Action

The rise of social media has brought about many advantages for society. It has become easier to communicate with your friends, reach larger audiences as a business, and it has even changed some people's lives forever. However, with these benefits comes many drawbacks. Social media can be a toxic place that causes worsened mental health, and even promotes hate speech.

There are a few way to take action. Below are two suggestions on how to get involved.

Call Your Senator

Congress has recently introduced a bill called Kids Online Safety Act of 2022. Ever since the integration of Social Media into everyday life, there has been an mental health epidemic amongst teenagers in America. KOSA aims to provide families with resources to combat these rising suicide rates and to maintain mental welbeing while online.
